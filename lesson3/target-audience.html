Audience

Our target audiences are those who want a straight to the point information about the upcoming weather. Some weather forecast sites can be very overwhelming as with the complicated wordings and explanations, but we made sure that the viewers can both get these information in the most understandable and fun way. The viewer can find entertaining real-life examples of weather interactions and how to save themeselves if ever a harsh weather is anticipated. We assure them that they have access to media like videos, graphical illustrations, and many more. We will also prepare a short and fun quizzes for those who want to test their knowledge about what to do when a disaster happen.

Audience's Profile

Kids or young people ages 8-12
Young adults and up ages 18 years old and older

Personas


Name: Clark Hunter

A nine-year-old kid from Idaho who's scared of thunder. One day he heard from the teachers in his school that a thunderstorm may happen a week after. He promised to his parents that he won't be calling them anymore if ever he hears a loud thunder because he is a big boys now. He wanted to prove that, so he went to browse the internet and was able to get to our website. He saw illustrations and videos about thunderstorms. He learned some tips on how to prepare himself in such situations.

Other scenarios:

  1. He told his parents that it is going to rain heavily during their family picnic on Saturday.
  2. He asked his parents to have a stargazing night on their yard because he saw from the website that a meteor shower is visible that night.


Name: Mike Garth

A salesman of plumbing supplies who walks around some neighborhoods to promote his company's products. He was planning his whole week and decided to check the weather for the upcoming days. He found out that a snowstorm will occur on a Monday afternoon, but he also read an article about how to spend a productive time when someone is stuck at home during a bad weather. He messaged his boss that he is going to spend a day of online selling since he can't go outside.

Other scenarios:

  1. He brought an extra shirt and lots of water after checking out the website and saw that it is going to be very hot the whole day.
