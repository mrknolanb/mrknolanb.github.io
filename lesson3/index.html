Site Purpose

The data collected from the present state of the atmosphere are used to provide accurate information on specific atmospheric changes for future weather guides. Unlike many other weather forecast sites, our goal is to provide better and easy-to-read content, relevant weather-related news, and appropriate guides for anticipated disasters.

We want viewers of all ages to easily make use of the information we offer. Simple illustrations with additional guides/explanations will be a game-changer for those who struggle to understand weather sites. Plus, up-to-date news regarding the weather condition on the user's location will be provided which makes it so much relevant. Whenever severe weather is anticipated, we want to make sure that users can have time to make enough preparations like packing emergency food, locating evacuation centers, and many more.
